Our Record Store

Our newly designed record store has been around since the early 1950's. We take great pride in presenting our customers with the absolute best, high quality records available on the open market. We offer a wide variety of services, such as buying and selling vinyl records, just to name a few. Here at the record store, we are very passionate about records, so whatever questions or concerns you may have, we can answer them! Please do not hesitate to come down if you need anything regarding our records or services. It has been our passion for the last 60 years to serve our loyal customers, and we look forward to many more years in service!
