What is Sony?

Sony is a Japenese based technological company. Their products range from various electronics, entertainment, and gaming services. The company is very well known, and their products can be found in almost every household. They manufacture a wide range of products, such as the Playstation, dishwashers, washer machines, dryers, microwaves, ovens, televisions, sound bars, and the list goes on. The parent company of 'Sony' has many different subdivisions, each responsible for a specific department. For example; Sony Pictures is responsible for creating movies and shows for customer entertainment, Sony Mobile is responsible for handling all of the company's mobile devices, such as phones and tablets, Sony Interactive Entertainment is responsible for creating and developing the video game and digital entertainment products for the company, just to name a few. Overall, Sony is a worldwide company that makes a variety of products from electonics to gaming. The company can be recognized by almost everyone and is prevelant in today's society.

History of Sony

Sony was created shortly after World War II. The company was founded by Masuaru Ibuka and Akio Morita. The first product to date was the TR-55 transitor radio. This was the first transitor radio to be made in the country of Japan. However, the company name wasn't 'Sony' until 1958. As time went on, the company kept designing and creating new ideas and technology. One of the many advancements in technology that we still use to this day, the Compact Disc (CD) was developed by Sony. From there, Sony wanted to keep pushing the barriers, and developed the PlayStation, a very successful gaming console, which would be furthered advanced by its successors, the PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, and the Playstation 4. The PlayStation 2 is still the best-selling home console to date. Shortly after, Sony would purchase other companies and begin their adventure into the entertainment industry. Now, present day, Sony is a successful brand that is recognized worldwide, in many different shapes and forms.
